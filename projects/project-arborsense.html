Arborsense
R&D | hardware prototype
June 19 - Present

Working with the engineering team at Arborsense, I aided in research and development of initial prototypes for arborsense's wearable BAC monitor.

I designed the housing for their sensors and molds used to cast silicone parts.

Silicone Mold

I designed and 3D printed a two-part mold for the silicone strap. The part was simple enough to be mainly made with a one-part open-faced mold, but an insert was needed to form a cavity for the sensor to fit in.
