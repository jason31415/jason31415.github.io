Scaling Greenhouse
architecture
Feb 20 - May 20

Working with a group of mechanical engineers, I was tasked to envision a greenhouse for Dabl's African Bead Museum in Detroit, MI. The design should invoke African design principles using logarithms and scaling forms.

Prototyping

Initial prototypes made using curved strips of paper.

The initial prototype was only curved along one profile. We wanted it to also be curved in the top-down view. Additionally, we decided to linearize the design so that flat planes of greenhouse glazing could be used, rather than curved pieces.

Final

Final model was made to scale and rendered in Fusion360.
